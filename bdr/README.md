# BDR Technical Challenge

Take-Home Challenge

Time: 5 days | Role: Business Development Representative

---

## Overview

This challenge evaluates your ability to quickly learn about Voidr, our market, and demonstrate strong communication and sales skills. You'll research our company, identify ideal prospects, and simulate a discovery call with a potential customer.

---

## What is Voidr?

Before starting the challenge, you need to deeply understand what Voidr does. Use the following resources:

- **Website:** https://voidr.co
- **LinkedIn:** https://linkedin.com/company/voidrco
- **Product pages:** Explore all sections of the website

Your understanding of Voidr will be evaluated throughout the challenge.

---

## Challenge Components

### Part 1: Prospect Research (Written Deliverable)

Create a list of **10 Brazilian companies** that would be ideal prospects for Voidr.

For each company, include:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company Name** | Official company name |
| **Segment** | Fintech, Healthtech, Bank, Insurance, E-commerce, etc. |
| **Why Good Fit** | Specific reasons why this company needs Voidr's solution |
| **Target Persona** | Job title of the person you would contact (e.g., QA Director, VP of Engineering) |
| **Approach Channel** | How you would reach them (LinkedIn, Email, Phone, Event, etc.) |
| **Pain Point / Trigger** | The specific problem or situation you would use to start the conversation |

**Format:** Deliver as a PDF, Google Sheets, or Notion document.

---

### Part 2: Discovery Call Simulation (Video Deliverable)

Record a video (maximum **10 minutes**) simulating a discovery call.

#### Scenario

You are a BDR at Voidr. A **QA Director from a fintech** scheduled a call with you after receiving your cold email. The fintech has:
- A mobile app and web platform with millions of users
- A growing engineering team struggling with release velocity
- Manual testing processes that are becoming a bottleneck
- Pressure from leadership to increase deployment frequency

#### What You Must Demonstrate

1. **Rapport Building**
   - Professional greeting and icebreaker
   - Set the agenda for the call

2. **Qualification Questions**
   - Use BANT, MEDDIC, or similar methodology
   - Ask about their current testing process
   - Understand their pain points and challenges
   - Identify decision-making process and timeline

3. **Value Proposition**
   - Present Voidr in the context of their needs
   - Connect our solution to their specific problems
   - Avoid generic pitches â€” be specific

4. **Next Steps**
   - Propose clear next steps
   - Schedule follow-up or demo with Account Executive
   - Professional closing

#### Video Guidelines

- Record using Loom, OBS, or any screen recorder
- You can use slides or just speak to camera
- Speak as if you're actually on a call with the prospect (simulate their responses)
- Show energy, confidence, and professionalism

---

### Part 3: AI Usage Documentation (Required)

You **must** use AI tools to complete this challenge. Document your AI usage in a separate section of your submission.

Include:

- **Which AI tools you used** (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, etc.)
- **How you used AI** for:
  - Researching Voidr and the QA market
  - Building the prospect list
  - Preparing the discovery call script
  - Any other aspect of the challenge
- **Example prompts** that were particularly effective
- **What you learned** about using AI for sales preparation

We're evaluating your ability to leverage AI strategically, not avoid it.

## Evaluation Criteria

| Dimension | What We Evaluate |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Research Depth** | How well you understand Voidr, our product, and the QA/testing market |
| **Strategic Thinking** | Quality of prospect selection and rationale for each choice |
| **Communication** | Clarity, articulation, energy, and presence in the video |
| **Sales Skills** | Qualification techniques, objection handling, and pitch delivery |
| **AI Usage** | Strategic and conscious use of AI tools, quality of documentation |

### What We're NOT Looking For

- Perfection: We value authentic communication over scripted performances
- Generic pitches: Show you understand Voidr specifically
- Memorized scripts: Natural conversation is more important
- AI-generated text without refinement: We want to see your thinking
